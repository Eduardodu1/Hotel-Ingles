About Us

FARM HOTEL CÓRREGO DO BOM JESUS

With colonial-style constructions, loom bedding and furniture from the rustic Minas Gerais line: Hotel Fazenda Córrego do Bom Jesus. For the best benefit of our clients, we make available a beautiful heated lounge with fireplace, verandas with direct view to the Pedra de São Domingos and a large yard where pets are kept. Among these animals are horses, cows, oxen, ducks, chickens, and others. Bringing in a playful way the whole idea of the countryside.


For fun we also have horseback rides, cart rides, trails to the Pedra de São Domingos, an extensive outdoor pool, space for children, and a beautiful leisure area for you and your family. We also offer a chapel to be used for festive events, such as weddings, baptisms, and also a large party room. We have been in business since 1992, always seeking to serve our customers in the best way, providing joy in every detail and making this experience unforgettable. Our focus is you, the client, as we bring you away from the dynamic daily life, calming and relaxing each one with the best that the country can offer.


structure of all the spaces in the Hotel was designed and implemented by our ancestors, coming from a long and traditional family culture; from where we glimpsed a niche that can, above all, bring joy to others. Our Farm Hotel is located on the road to Pedra de São Domingos, Km 16,5, in the city of Córrego do Bom Jesus, Minas Gerais. Located 6 Km from Cambuí, and 155 Km from São Paulo capital.


Get to know our Hotel Fazenda Córrego do Bom Jesus. In the colors and flavors, a journey through country life in the richest traditions of Minas Gerais. Córrego do Bom Jesus: those who know you will never forget you!
